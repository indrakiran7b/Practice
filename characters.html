One Piece Characters

Monkey D. Luffy

The captain of the Straw Hat Pirates, Luffy is a cheerful and determined young man with the power of the Gum-Gum Fruit, allowing him to stretch his body like rubber.

Roronoa Zoro

The first mate of the Straw Hat Pirates, Zoro is a skilled swordsman who aims to become the world's greatest swordsman. He fights using the Three Sword Style.

Nami

The navigator of the Straw Hat Pirates, Nami is a skilled cartographer and thief with a dream to draw a map of the entire world.

Usopp

The sniper of the Straw Hat Pirates, Usopp is a talented marksman and inventor known for his elaborate lies and stories.

Sanji

The cook of the Straw Hat Pirates, Sanji is a master chef and powerful fighter who uses a unique kicking-based combat style.

Tony Tony Chopper

The doctor of the Straw Hat Pirates, Chopper is a reindeer who gained human abilities after eating the Human-Human Fruit.

Nico Robin

The archaeologist of the Straw Hat Pirates, Robin has the power to sprout additional body parts and is driven by her desire to uncover the world's hidden history.

Franky

The shipwright of the Straw Hat Pirates, Franky is a cyborg with superhuman strength and the ability to modify his body with various weapons and tools.
